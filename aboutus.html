  WELCOME TO EKA HOTEL NAIROBI

   Conveniently located along Mombasa Road with easy access to both Jomo
   Kenyatta International Airport, Wilson Airport and next to the Nairobi
   National Park, Eka offers the perfect choice hotel for both business
   and leisure traveler.
